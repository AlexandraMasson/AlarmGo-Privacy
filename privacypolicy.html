Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Overview

AlarmGo ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

Information We Do NOT Collect

AlarmGo does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The App:

  • Does not collect user analytics
  • Does not track your location
  • Does not access your contacts
  • Does not store your calendar data on any servers
  • Does not share any information with third parties
  • Does not use cookies or tracking technologies

Calendar Access

The App requires access to your device's calendar to:

  • Display your upcoming events
  • Set local alarm notifications for selected events

This calendar data:

  • Remains entirely on your device
  • Is never transmitted to any servers
  • Is never shared with anyone
  • Is only accessed while the app is in use

Alarm Notifications

Alarms are stored locally on your device using iOS's built-in notification system. We do not have access to these notifications once scheduled.

Subscription Information

If you purchase a subscription:

  • Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store
  • We do not have access to your payment information
  • Apple may share basic transaction information (purchase date, subscription status) but no personal payment details

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The App is intended for general audiences.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date.
